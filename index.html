Why Sargam

Other apps want to
fix you. We walk with you.

Most mental health apps were designed to treat a problem. Sargam was designed to be a companion for life. Here is what that actually means.

01

You never have to explain yourself twice

Every other AI resets when you close the app. Sargam carries your story forward. The job that stressed you out. The relationship that drained you. The thing you were afraid to say out loud. It picks up exactly where you left off.

02

It listens before it says anything

Therapy apps give you CBT exercises. Meditation apps give you breathing techniques. Sargam gives you space to feel heard first. No worksheets. No 5-step plans. Just someone sitting with you in it before trying to solve anything.

03

Always there, no appointment needed

Hard moments don't wait for a scheduled session. Sargam is available the moment you need it. Middle of the night, lunch break, between meetings. No wait time, no voicemail, no calendar link.

04

Built for everyday life, not just breaking points

Most tools activate only when you're in crisis. Sargam is for everything in between. The frustrating day. The awkward family dinner. The Sunday dread. The loneliness that has no clean reason. You don't need to be falling apart to talk.

05

Lives where you already are, on WhatsApp

You already have WhatsApp. Sargam lives there. No new app to download or learn. No onboarding flow. No dashboard. Just a number you can text, any time, from wherever you already are.
